Scarborough to Fremantle — one move, seven nights, a lot of quokkas

30 Aug – 6 Sep 2026 Arrive 4:15pm · Depart 5:20pm Scarborough 30/8 → 2/9 Fremantle / Coogee 2/9 → 6/9
Strawberry picking

Farms near Gnangara/Wanneroo vary week to week — check the farm's Facebook page the night before Day 3, and have the chocolate co. or a playground as backup.

Busselton Jetty

3 Sep falls in the reduced May–Sept hours: Interpretive Centre 9:30am–4:15pm, Jetty Train 10am–4pm, Underwater Observatory tours 10am–3pm — all weather-dependent. Only pre-book if you can commit to the timing; otherwise foreshore + a walk out on the jetty is the low-risk plan.

Rottnest ferry

Book the Fremantle–Rottnest ferry ahead of time (about 25–30 min each way) and pack windbreakers — late winter/early spring can be breezy on the island.

One adjustment from the original spreadsheet: the plan document had you moving from Scarborough to Coogee on Day 5, the same day as the Busselton trip. Since you're actually checking out of Scarborough on 2 Sep, I split that into two lighter days — Day 4 is just the move, and Day 5 is a Busselton day trip out of Fremantle/Coogee with no accommodation change. It also shaves roughly 15–20 minutes off the Busselton drive each way, since Coogee sits south of the city, closer to the South West. Penguin Island has also been dropped from Day 7 — the landing is closed for the winter nesting season on 5 Sep, so the day is now a straightforward Rockingham foreshore visit instead.
